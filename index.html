Some Important Information Before You Start:

If this is your first visit to The Bean Counter's Books, then you'll need to register to proceed. Don't worry, registration is quite easy -- you get to fill out a form. (How exciting is that!)

Just click on "Get Started By Registering" and you're away.

Please Note: The Bean Counter's Books is designed to use the Open Financial Exchange -- OFX -- file format. This is an international standard that has been in use by Financial Institutions for over twenty years. Some Banks, such as Westpac, haven't gotten into line with these International standards yet. If your bank doesn't allow you to download your data in the OFX format, then The Bean Counter's Books is NOT FOR YOU. There is little point in you continuing.
